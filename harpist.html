Harpist

Chris Lindgren, Saskatoon's most seasoned harpist has been performing with her harp since 1983. She has played for audiences from B.C. to Ontario, at events such as the Canadian Folk Harp Festival (Toronto), University of Winnipeg Concert Series and the Regina Folk Festival. Locally she has played for hundreds of weddings, receptions, banquets, and concerts.

Her extensive repertoire includes timeless medieval and Renaissance melodies, elegant classical pieces, to lively Celtic tunes.

She can be heard on her latest CD "Harp for Dessert". Her first album, "Winter Harp" (1991) , is no longer available.

Chris can provide her own sound system for most events.

Available on very short notice, but a week's notice or more would be appreciated.

Types of Performances

  • Background music for receptions, teas, etc.
  • Music for wedding ceremonies.
  • Feature concert programs available.
